About Us

Pup Talk is a teen-led platform where stories and topics related to volunteering at
Guide Dogs for the Blind is shared with the GDB community and the world.

Kaiser Williams

Founder

A Junior at Bellevue High School raising his 3rd guide dog puppy. Raised 2 guide dogs back in Japan, and currently raising his first guide dog puppy for Guide Dogs for the Blind. An avid programmer (frontend), basketball player, Officer for BHS's National Honor Society, Head of Marketing for BHS TEDx club, Co-founder of CW Academy, who loves to learn new things every day!

Titan is a guide dog puppy in training raised by Kaiser and his family. He is an outgoing male black lab!
